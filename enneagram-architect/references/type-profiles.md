# Enneagram Type Profiles

## The Three Centers

**Head Center (5, 6, 7):** Governed by anxiety/fear; mental process dominant
**Heart Center (2, 3, 4):** Governed by shame/hostility; feeling dominant  
**Body Center (8, 9, 1):** Governed by anger/rage; relating/action dominant

---

## Type One: The Reformer

**Center:** Body (anger—expressed as resentment)
**Core Fear:** Being corrupt, wrong, defective, imperfect
**Core Desire:** To be good, have integrity, be balanced
**Core Distortion:** Harsh judgment of self and others
**Core Image:** "I am righteous, reasonable, sensible"

**Focus of Attention:** What's wrong; errors, mistakes, imperfection
**Survival Strategy:** Being right, proper, correct

**Wings:**
- 1w9 (The Idealist): More detached, philosophical, cooler
- 1w2 (The Advocate): More interpersonal, helpful, warmer

**Movement:**
- Under Stress → Four: Becomes moody, irrational, melancholic, self-absorbed
- Growth → Seven: Becomes spontaneous, joyful, accepting of imperfection

**Communication Markers:**
- Speaking Style: Instructive, precise; uses "should/ought/must"
- Blind Spots: Own rigidity; how criticism affects others
- Distortion Filters: Hears criticism when none intended

**Common Pinches:** Others being sloppy, irresponsible; rules broken; being told they're wrong

---

## Type Two: The Helper

**Center:** Heart (shame)
**Core Fear:** Being unwanted, unloved, unnecessary
**Core Desire:** To feel loved, to be needed
**Core Distortion:** Manipulation through helping
**Core Image:** "I am loving, caring, needed"

**Focus of Attention:** Others' needs; how to be indispensable
**Survival Strategy:** Being needed, helpful, essential

**Wings:**
- 2w1 (The Servant): More idealistic, controlled, task-oriented
- 2w3 (The Host/Hostess): More image-conscious, charming, ambitious

**Movement:**
- Under Stress → Eight: Becomes aggressive, dominating, demanding
- Growth → Four: Becomes self-nurturing, emotionally honest

**Communication Markers:**
- Speaking Style: Warm, flattering; uses "we" and "us"
- Blind Spots: Own needs; how their "help" controls others
- Distortion Filters: Hears rejection in independence

**Common Pinches:** Not being appreciated; being called manipulative; help being rejected

---

## Type Three: The Achiever

**Center:** Heart (shame)
**Core Fear:** Being worthless, without inherent value
**Core Desire:** To feel valuable and worthwhile
**Core Distortion:** Deception (of self and others about true self)
**Core Image:** "I am successful, accomplished, effective"

**Focus of Attention:** Goals, tasks, image, success
**Survival Strategy:** Achievement, winning, looking good

**Wings:**
- 3w2 (The Charmer): More interpersonal, warm, helpful
- 3w4 (The Professional): More introspective, artistic, emotional depth

**Movement:**
- Under Stress → Nine: Becomes disengaged, apathetic, checked out
- Growth → Six: Becomes cooperative, committed to others, loyal

**Communication Markers:**
- Speaking Style: Confident, goal-oriented, efficiency-focused
- Blind Spots: Own feelings; sacrificing authenticity for success
- Distortion Filters: Hears judgment about their worth

**Common Pinches:** Public failure; being ignored; being called a fraud; inefficiency

---

## Type Four: The Individualist

**Center:** Heart (shame)
**Core Fear:** Having no identity or personal significance
**Core Desire:** To find themselves and their significance
**Core Distortion:** Envy, chronic melancholy
**Core Image:** "I am unique, deep, sensitive"

**Focus of Attention:** What's missing; own emotional landscape
**Survival Strategy:** Being unique, special, different

**Wings:**
- 4w3 (The Aristocrat): More ambitious, image-aware, socially engaged
- 4w5 (The Bohemian): More withdrawn, intellectual, unconventional

**Movement:**
- Under Stress → Two: Becomes over-involved, clingy, people-pleasing
- Growth → One: Becomes objective, principled, disciplined

**Communication Markers:**
- Speaking Style: Expressive, dramatic; rich emotional vocabulary
- Blind Spots: How specialness alienates; addiction to melancholy
- Distortion Filters: Hears superficiality in others

**Common Pinches:** Being told they're ordinary; being misunderstood; emotional unavailability

---

## Type Five: The Investigator

**Center:** Head (anxiety)
**Core Fear:** Being useless, helpless, incapable
**Core Desire:** To be capable and competent
**Core Distortion:** Avarice (hoarding knowledge, time, energy)
**Core Image:** "I am perceptive, wise, self-sufficient"

**Focus of Attention:** Knowledge, information, understanding systems
**Survival Strategy:** Withdrawal, conserving energy, knowing

**Wings:**
- 5w4 (The Iconoclast): More creative, emotionally intense, unconventional
- 5w6 (The Problem Solver): More analytical, loyal, team-oriented

**Movement:**
- Under Stress → Seven: Becomes hyperactive, scattered, escapist
- Growth → Eight: Becomes self-confident, decisive, engaged

**Communication Markers:**
- Speaking Style: Precise, analytical, information-focused
- Blind Spots: Own emotional needs; how withdrawal affects relationships
- Distortion Filters: Hears emotional demands as intrusions

**Common Pinches:** Invasions of privacy; being called incompetent; unexpected emotional demands

---

## Type Six: The Loyalist

**Center:** Head (anxiety)
**Core Fear:** Being without support and guidance
**Core Desire:** To have security and support
**Core Distortion:** Cowardice (phobic) or counter-phobia (reckless)
**Core Image:** "I am loyal, reliable, prepared"

**Focus of Attention:** Danger, threats, what could go wrong
**Survival Strategy:** Vigilance, loyalty, strategic thinking

**Wings:**
- 6w5 (The Defender): More introverted, cerebral, independent
- 6w7 (The Buddy): More extroverted, playful, engaging

**Movement:**
- Under Stress → Three: Becomes competitive, arrogant, image-focused
- Growth → Nine: Becomes relaxed, optimistic, trusting

**Communication Markers:**
- Speaking Style: Questioning, devil's advocate, worst-case scenarios
- Blind Spots: How suspicion creates betrayal they fear
- Distortion Filters: Hears hidden agendas in straightforward communication

**Common Pinches:** Betrayal of trust; unpredictability; being called paranoid; ambiguous authority

---

## Type Seven: The Enthusiast

**Center:** Head (anxiety)
**Core Fear:** Being deprived, trapped, in pain
**Core Desire:** To be satisfied and content
**Core Distortion:** Gluttony (for experience)
**Core Image:** "I am fun, spontaneous, versatile"

**Focus of Attention:** Possibilities, options, positive future
**Survival Strategy:** Keeping options open, staying positive, avoiding pain

**Wings:**
- 7w6 (The Entertainer): More engaging, loyal, responsible
- 7w8 (The Realist): More assertive, materialistic, tough-minded

**Movement:**
- Under Stress → One: Becomes perfectionistic, critical, irritable
- Growth → Five: Becomes focused, profound, engaged with depth

**Communication Markers:**
- Speaking Style: Enthusiastic, fast, jumps between topics
- Blind Spots: How they avoid depth; impact when they get bored and leave
- Distortion Filters: Hears limitations as oppression

**Common Pinches:** Being trapped or limited; forced commitment; others being "negative"

---

## Type Eight: The Challenger

**Center:** Body (anger)
**Core Fear:** Being harmed or controlled by others
**Core Desire:** To protect themselves, be in control
**Core Distortion:** Lust (for intensity)
**Core Image:** "I am strong, powerful, protective"

**Focus of Attention:** Power, control, justice, who's in charge
**Survival Strategy:** Being strong, controlling environment, protecting self/others

**Wings:**
- 8w7 (The Maverick): More energetic, extroverted, enterprising
- 8w9 (The Bear): More grounded, receptive, steady

**Movement:**
- Under Stress → Five: Becomes secretive, fearful, withdrawn
- Growth → Two: Becomes open-hearted, caring, protective through love

**Communication Markers:**
- Speaking Style: Direct, commanding, declarative
- Blind Spots: How intensity intimidates; own vulnerability
- Distortion Filters: Hears weakness as pathetic

**Common Pinches:** Being controlled or manipulated; injustice; weakness exposed; vulnerability shown

---

## Type Nine: The Peacemaker

**Center:** Body (anger—most repressed)
**Core Fear:** Loss, separation, fragmentation
**Core Desire:** Inner stability, peace of mind
**Core Distortion:** Sloth (toward own agenda/priorities)
**Core Image:** "I am easygoing, peaceful, accepting"

**Focus of Attention:** Others' positions; maintaining harmony
**Survival Strategy:** Going along, merging with others, avoiding conflict

**Wings:**
- 9w8 (The Referee): More assertive, body-aware, grounded
- 9w1 (The Dreamer): More idealistic, orderly, detached

**Movement:**
- Under Stress → Six: Becomes anxious, worried, reactive
- Growth → Three: Becomes self-developing, energetic, focused

**Communication Markers:**
- Speaking Style: Agreeable, meandering, uses qualifiers
- Blind Spots: Own anger; how passivity creates conflict; own preferences
- Distortion Filters: Hears demands in requests

**Common Pinches:** Being forced to choose sides; direct conflict; being overlooked
